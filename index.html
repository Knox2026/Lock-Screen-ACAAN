Confirmation Handshake (v9.0)

When you perform the Single Tap, the app will verbally say "Voice Mode Activated." This confirms your Bluetooth earpiece is connected and the mic is live without needing to look at the screen.

Core Logic & ACAAN

Stack Offset: Select your current top card so the app calculates from the actual position.

Shift Logic:

  • :XX AM: Cut cards from TOP.
  • :XX PM: Cut cards from BOTTOM.
  • :00 AM / Sunday 24: Card is already at target number.

The Verification Crib
