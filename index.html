Lock Screen ACAAN

A high-fidelity, stealth utility designed for modern performance. This Progressive Web App (PWA) mimics a standard smartphone lock screen to provide covert data for Any Card At Any Number (ACAAN) effects.

🚀 Key Features

  • Innocent Mode: Displays actual system time/date until a calculation is triggered.
  • Voice-Triggered Logic: Listens for a customizable "Trigger Word" (Default: Focus).
  • Multi-Stack Support: Redford, Mnemonica, Aronson, Memorandum, Particle, and Si Stebbins.
  • Dual-Mode Feedback: Visual (screen) and Auditory (whispered via Speaker/Bluetooth).
  • Wake Lock: Prevents the screen from dimming or sleeping during your set.

⚙️ Settings Explained

Device Look: Switches typography between iOS (Bold), Android (Light), and Samsung (Medium).

Clock Color: Choose White, Ice Blue, or Warm Sand to match your lighting.

Current Top Card (Offset): If the deck is cut, select the card currently on top so the app knows your exact stack starting point.

Voice Trigger Word: The specific word that tells the app to "Open its ears" for a card and number.

Listen Timeout: How many seconds the app stays in "Listening Mode" after hearing the trigger word.

📋 Calculation Logic (The "Crib")

The Clock

Minutes (01–26): The number of cards to be shifted.

AM: Cut cards from the TOP of the deck to the bottom.

PM: Cut cards from the BOTTOM of the deck to the top.

Shift 0: Displayed as [Real Hour]:00 AM.

The Date Line (Bottom Card Verification)

The date line verifies the card that should be at the Bottom after the cut:

  • Monday: Clubs (♣) | Tuesday: Hearts (♥)
  • Wednesday: Spades (♠) | Thursday: Diamonds (♦)
  • Number: Ace=1, Jack=11, Queen=12, King=13, and 2-10.
  • Home State: Displays Sunday [Month] 24 if the card is already at the target position.

🎭 Performance Walkthrough

Phase 1: The Setup

Open the app (shows real time). Set the phone down and Single Tap. The app is now "hot," awake, and listening in the background.

Phase 2: The Input

Incorporate the trigger word into your patter: "Just Focus on the Seven of Hearts and the number Fourteen."

Phase 3: The Secret Read

Glance at the clock (e.g., 5:07 PM). This tells you to cut 7 cards from the bottom. If using a Bluetooth earpiece, the app will whisper the instructions to you automatically.

Phase 4: The Clean Up

While the spectator is counting, Double Tap the screen. The evidence vanishes, replaced by the actual current time.
