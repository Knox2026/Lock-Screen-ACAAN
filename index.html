User Manual

Overview

This app functions as a covert Crib-u-lator for the Redford Playing Card Stack. It masquerades as a smartphone lock screen while performing ACAAN calculations via voice recognition.

Gestures

  • Long Press (2.5s): Opens the Config Menu.
  • Double Tap: Resets the clock to the "Zero State" (12:00 AM).
  • Single Tap: Requests Fullscreen and wakes the Voice Engine.

The Shortest Path Logic

The app calculates the fastest way to get the target card to the target position:

  • :XX AM = Cut XX cards from TOP to BOTTOM.
  • :XX PM = Cut XX cards from BOTTOM to TOP.

Verification (The Date Line)

The "Date" identifies the card that will be at the BOTTOM after your cut:

  • Monday: Clubs (♣)
  • Tuesday: Hearts (♥)
  • Wednesday: Spades (♠)
  • Thursday: Diamonds (♦)
  • Day Number: Matches card value (1=A, 11=J, 12=Q, 13=K).

Voice Commands

The app listens for your Trigger Word. Once heard, you have 5 seconds to say a Number (1-52) and a Card Name (e.g., "Eleven", "Jack of Spades").
